## Props
### Spotlight Card
               
| Name               | Default                   | Description                                              
| ------------------ | ------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| **as**             | **div**                   | **React.ElementType**<div>The element type to render the component as (e.g., `div`, `button`, `section`)</div>                  
| **className**      | **—**                     | **String**<div>The class name of the spotlight component.</div>
| **size**           | **100**                   | **Number**<div>Gradient size</div>
| **from**           | **rgba(255,255,255,0.4)** | **String**<div>Starting color stop</div>
| **via**            | **—**                     | **String**<div>Middle color stop</div>
| **to**             | **transparent**           | **String**<div>Ending color stop</div>
| **opacity**        | **100**                   | **Number**<div>opacity of the effect</div>
| **isAround**       | **false**                 | **Boolean**<div>Whether the spotlight is surrounded by a background</div>
| **mode**           | **before**                | **before \| aflter**<div>Highlight display mode</div>
